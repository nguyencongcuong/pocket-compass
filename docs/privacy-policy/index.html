Privacy Policy

Pocket Compass · Last updated: 3 April 2026

This policy describes how Pocket Compass (“the App”), developed by Cuong Nguyen, handles information when you use the mobile application on iOS and Android. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

Summary

  • The App is a digital compass and does not require you to create an account.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Sensor and location data used for compass and declination features are processed on your device to operate the App. We do not operate our own servers to collect this data from the App.
  • The App does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in its current dependency set.

Information the App uses

The App may access the following, depending on your device and permissions:

  • Device location (when in use) — Requested so the App can estimate magnetic declination and show headings relative to true north where supported. Location is used for this calculation; it is not used to build a profile of you for marketing. Processing is performed on the device as part of normal App operation.
  • Compass / motion sensors — Used to determine heading for the compass display. This occurs on your device.
  • App version metadata — Read locally (for example, to display version information in the App UI). This stays on your device.

Permissions (Android)

On Android, the App may declare permissions including:

  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION and ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — for the location-based declination feature described above.
  • INTERNET — May be present in the manifest (for example, as required by the platform or dependencies). The App is not designed to upload your compass or location data to developer-controlled servers.

Permissions (iOS)

On iOS, the App requests location when in use where needed for declination / true-north related functionality, consistent with the system permission prompt.

Third parties

Apple and Google provide the operating system and may process data according to their own policies. The App relies on standard platform APIs and open-source libraries bundled in the application (for example, for sensors, permissions, and geolocation). Review your device privacy settings to manage location and sensor access.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
